CEO-Maps.me

A Mapathon is a coordinated mapping event during which the participants try to mark as many new places on the map as they can. At MapsMe, we believe that the users can be a tremendous asset to help us deepening our mapping network. We organize Mapathons all around the globe with our growing community. And we need your help! So if you're feeling adventurous, all you need is your camera and your joie de vivre to get in our Mapathon Bus for an unforgettable experience.
